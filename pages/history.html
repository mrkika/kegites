History & Membership

Our Origin

The Kegites Club was founded in 1962 at the University of Ife (now Obafemi Awolowo University) by visionary students to promote African heritage and unity.

Purpose and Growth

The club fosters African traditions, camaraderie, and excellence. It has grown to many Nigerian universities and beyond.

Common Misconceptions

Some wrongly associate the club with secret societies or cultism. In truth, the Kegites Club is cultural and social, emphasizing unity and integrity.

Notable Members

Many distinguished Nigerians have been members, including prominent academics, politicians, and cultural leaders who embody the club's values.
